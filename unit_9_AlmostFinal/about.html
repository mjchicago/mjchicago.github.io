About Us:
Globetrotter Travel was designed with a unique premise - to serve "serious travelers" - those travelers who have seen a good amount of the world and thus have a different outlook on travel. You enjoy having unique, interesting experiences in the places you visit. You also want to travel in the company of others who are well-traveled, and to connect with others who have great travel stories to share. If this sounds intiguing to you, Globetrotter Travel is the right travel partner for you!
