2017 Trips:

Peru:
Our Peru journey will take you to a country like no other - incredible scenery, historic Inca ruins and sacred sites, delicious food and some of the most hospitable local people you will ever encounter.

Brazil:
Globetrotter Travel's Brazil adventure includes an Amazon river cruise that will take you to a place so remote that you likely will not see anyone for days! You are likely to see, however, see gorgeous birds, pink dolphins, caimans, sloths, monkeys and if you are lucky, a snake or two.

Spain:
Our trip to Spain will take you to the authentic southern city of Seville, a great city for walking and exploring great sites, and sampling a flamenco performance at night. We will also travel to Granada, a lovely city and home of the Alhambra.
